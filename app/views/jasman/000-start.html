Organising a data share between government departments

Before you begin the process of creating a data share, we recommend that you have the following items prepared:

  • The ability to clealry express why you need another department to allow access to their data. You must be able to describe the beneficial outcomes that will be created by your use of the data resource.
  • A clear understanding of the data you wish to share, including any legal or regulatory requirements that apply.
  • The necessary permissions to share the data, including any relevant data protection or security clearances.

There is quite a lot to creating a successful data share request, and the process can take some time. You will get what you need more quickly if you prepare well. Please review the steps on the right to learn about what you will need
