Get started

This service is for people who work in government organisations.

It allows you to find and request government data that is not available to the public.

Visit data.gov.uk to find data that’s already in the public domain, and can be accessed by anyone.

Create an account

You need an account in order to access the data catalogue and other features in the Data Marketplace.

To set up your account, you must:

  • be a government employee
  • have a government email address

Go to the sign in page, enter your email address and continue.

You’ll then go through your regular government sign-in process

You can also request additional roles for your account to approve data requests or publish metadata records.

Start using the Data Marketplace

Once you’ve signed in, you can explore the data catalogue and find data you’re interested in.
