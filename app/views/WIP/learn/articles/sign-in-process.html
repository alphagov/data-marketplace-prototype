Sign-in process

Access to some features and data in the Data Marketplace, for example to start a data share request, requires you to sign in to verify that you’re a government employee. You can sign in at any time by clicking the Sign in link at the top of the page. Otherwise you will be prompted to sign in when you try to access a feature that’s only available to authorised users.

To sign in, you'll use your government email address and go through your standard sign-in process, for example an authentication code sent to your phone or using an authentication app. Our sign in process uses the government single sign on service.

When you first sign in to the Data Marketplace, you'll be invited to complete your profile by choosing your organisation from a drop-down list and select your primary skill from a list of available options.

Once you've set up your profile and are signed in to the Data Marketplace, you'll see an Account link at the top of the page. From there, you can view your profile which will show your:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Organisation
  • Role
